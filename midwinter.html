2025 Event Notes

  • When we arrived we paid a gold coin, we recieved a Song book and informed where our group was seated
  • Only seats were provided, arranged around a center stage of 5 people
  • We played around 15 songs from the book (one after another) before some of the groups played an item, either seated or standing (smaller groups could come to the front)
  • We played a few more songs before breaking for afternoon tea
  • After break we had more groups play an item then special guests play
  • We then finished the song book off before heading out

  • Dont forget your Sheet Music Stand
  • The Hall gets quite hot, so no need for lots of layers
