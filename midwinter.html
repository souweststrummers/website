Darfield Midwinter Strum

Saturday, 5 July 2025, 1pm - 4:30pm
Darfield Recreation Centre, 92 North Terrace, Darfield

Kia ora ukulele friends!

Plans are evolving for midwinter ukulele fun day on 5th July!

Please share on your Facebook page or email list

This year we are encouraging all the Ukulele groups to get everyone from their group to perform together in an item. We are inviting the following groups;


Whos attending

Rangiora Ukulele Group
Ashburton Group
Waimate G Strings
New Brighton Group
Cashmere Group
Papanui Group
Geraldine Ukulele Group
Sou’west Strummers
Darfield Ukulele Group

Also inviting;
The Velvet Gloves
Pic n Mix
Balcairn Rovers

This year we will arrange the seating so groups can perform where they are seated. There will a microphone (improved one!) and basic amp at the front also.

Sorry no soloists or other small groups due to limited time at the Rec Centre event but there is an Open mic planned for the morning and possibly the evening. Please email me if you are keen for this.


The plan

10.30am - 12 pm Open Mic at the Grain and Grape in Darfield
1pm - 4.30 Darfield Recreation Centre ( doors open 12.30 ready to start at 1pm.
1pm / 1.30 Big strum along
1.30 -2.15 Group performances
2.15 - 2.45 Big Strum along
2.45 - 3.15 afternoon tea
3.15 - 4 Group Performances
4 - 4.30 Final Strum along


See you there!

That’s all for now. Please bring your Darfield Uke Midwinter Strum book from last year - will have some available on the day. No plate to bring this year, donation entry.
Looking forward to seeing most of you soon.
