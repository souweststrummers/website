Welcome to Sou'West Strummers

Sou’west Strummers is mainly a ukulele group with guitar and a few other instruments included. All players are welcome, even if you've only just started learning.

Join Us

Every Thursday night, 7pm – 9pm

The Venue

Golden Mile Tavern and Restaurant, 2 Trents Road
Restaurant entrance, rear of building
Golden Mile Tavern, Restaurant Menu

Let's Jam!

When you arrive, grab any of the available tables, or see if there are some free spots with other players.

We go from table to table asking which song you want to play. You can find all the songs here Songs

We take a break at 8pm for a cup of coffee and a chat.

Please bring a gold coin with you to help cover various events throughout the year.
