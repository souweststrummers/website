Welcome to Sou'West Strummers

Sou’west Strummers is mainly a ukulele group with guitar and a few other instruments included.
All players are welcome, even if you've only started learning

Join Us

We meet every Thursday night, 7.00 p.m. – 9.00 p.m.
Please bring a gold coin with you

The Venue

Golden Mile Tavern and Restaurant
2 Trents Rd – off the main highway
(right round the back for the restaurant entrance)

Let's Jam!

When you arrive, grab any of the available tables, or see if theres some free spots with others
If you want something to eat, check out the menu - Golden Mile Tavern, Restaurant Menu
We'll then go from table to table asking which song you want to play, songs can be found here - Songs
We'll take a break at 8.00 p.m. for a cup of coffee or a chat.
