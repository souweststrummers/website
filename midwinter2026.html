2026 Darfield Spring Strum

Lets Strum!

  • Entry is a gold coin, you will then receieve the all new song book for this year! then head into the hall and locate our group
  • We'll play around 15 songs from the book (one after another) before some of the groups play an item, either seated or standing (smaller groups can come to the front)
  • We'll played a few more songs before breaking for afternoon tea
  • After break we'll have some more groups play an item then our special guests
  • We'll then finished the song book off before heading out

  • Note only seats are provided - so don't forget your Sheet Music Stand
  • The Hall gets quite hot!
